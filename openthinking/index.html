Questions parents ask

The things you're wondering.

all_inclusive How does "unlimited" tutoring actually work?
You book a drop-in session and stay as long as your student needs that day — there's no 50-minute timer and no "we're out of time" right when it's clicking. Some students come for an hour, some stay through a tough problem set. Understanding sets the pace, not a stopwatch.
functions What grades and subjects do you cover?
Math and physics only — and we go deep in both. Grades 1–12 plus college and AP: from elementary math and pre-algebra through Calculus III, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations, Statistics, and high-school, AP, and college Physics.
payments How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on whether you want unlimited drop-in tutoring or a term-based class, and how often your student comes in. The simplest way is a free consult — we'll find exactly where your student is and walk you through the options with no pressure. Start with the form below.
diversity_2 Do you support students with learning differences or autism?
Yes. Students who learn differently are genuinely welcome here — the no-clock, one-on-one format is often exactly what helps. Tell us what works best for your child in the form and we'll make sure their teacher knows before the first session.
verified_user You mention AI — is my child's information safe?
The teaching is always done by a real teacher, in the room. We use AI only behind the scenes to help draft your progress notes and handle scheduling, and it works with code-names — your child's full name is never shared with any AI tool. Records stay in our own private accounts, each family only sees their own child's reports, and nothing reaches you until a real teacher has reviewed it.
location_on Are you in-person, and where?
In-person only — that's the whole point. We're at 6850 Regional St, Suite 200, Dublin, CA 94568, founded and taught by UC Berkeley math & physics grads.
